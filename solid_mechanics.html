Stresses and Equilibrium

Consider a three-dimensional body with volume V and surface S as shown in figure. The x,y and z axis are shown. The displacement on some part of the boundary is specified. Under the action of loading, the body deforms. The deformation of a point x is given by u=[u,v,w]T. The loads acting on the body can be distributed force per unit area T or distributed force per unit volume f. The traction force components are represented as T=[Tx, Ty, Tz]T. The body force components are represented as f=[fx, fy, fz]T.
